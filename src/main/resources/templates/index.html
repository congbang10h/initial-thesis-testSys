Welcome to the new generation of Testing System

Currently, there are several popular testing systems, for examples, embedded tests in Sakai [14] and Moodle [11], TestMaker [8] and TAO [9]. However, none of them enable learning-quality assessment, i.e., to infer the satisfaction of the educational program’s objectives and course’s learning outcomes from the results of tests. This kind of learning-quality assessment is best described in ABET [6] and CDIO [4].
